# Domestic Gal Social Texture

## Call This When

Before writing Chinese visual novel scenes that risk sounding generic, weightless, or translated from a vague Japanese-school template.

## Study Base

Primary study object: The Hungry Lamb: Traveling in the Late Ming Dynasty.

Supporting public samples and craft notes:

- official Zero Creation developer posts about historical pressure, ordinary-person POV, and characters growing from social/historical material
- CnGal essay on experience-centered script design in The Hungry Lamb
- CnGal notes on Galgame textbox rhythm and limited-reading POV
- public Tricolour Lovestory official site samples showing Chinese campus speech,口癖, stutters, joke timing, and short textbox framing

## Goal

Make dialogue grow from local social pressure. A line should carry class, job, age, debt, route cost, and the speaker's available escape routes.

## Procedure

1. Name the social machine in the scene: station, school, factory, village, family table, police desk, ward, dorm, office, neighborhood committee.
2. Give the machine a mundane tool: form, seal, register, meal card, group chat, work order, attendance sheet, camera, overtime sheet, medicine box.
3. Define what happens after the scene if the machine receives the character's name.
4. Give each speaker one material want and one face-saving lie.
5. Put local, occupational, or age-specific speech into pressure lines, but do not turn it into catchphrase cosplay.
6. Let choices change a social relation, not only an affection value: who can call whom, who owes a witness, who becomes responsible on paper.
7. End the scene on a changed object or procedure. Do not end with an essay sentence.

## Line Rules

- Use short concrete nouns:票根,章,工牌,钥匙,群消息,登记栏,值班电话.
- Let a character avoid the moral noun by naming the practical consequence.
- Let the protagonist's kindness cost them something measurable.
- Give the vulnerable character a sharp edge; do not make them only可怜.
- Let the scene remember local bureaucracy: a call gets logged, a name enters a table, a file can be forwarded.

## Output Fields

- social_machine
- mundane_tool
- name_consequence
- material_want
- face_saving_lie
- relation_cost
- changed_object_or_procedure

## Failure Signs

- the scene could happen in any city, any school, or any country
- conflict is only personality conflict
- choices are just kind / unkind
- everyone speaks in clean literary Mandarin
- the protagonist's help has no workplace or social cost
- the vulnerable character has no leverage
