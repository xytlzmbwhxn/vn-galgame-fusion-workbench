# Scene Question / Answer

## Call This When

Every time a scene card or scene draft brief is created.

## Inputs

- scene location and time
- protagonist's immediate job
- opposing person, rule, object, or deadline
- expected state change

## Procedure

1. Write the scene question as a concrete yes/no or either/or pressure.
2. Write the answer before drafting dialogue.
3. Check that the answer creates a new problem, debt, clue, or loss.
4. Remove any beat that does not push the question toward the answer.

## Output Fields

- `scene_question`
- `scene_answer`
- `dramatic_turn`
- `sequel_decision`

## Failure Signs

- the scene is only mood
- the scene can be summarized as "they talk"
- the ending does not alter the next scene
- the opening question and ending answer do not match
