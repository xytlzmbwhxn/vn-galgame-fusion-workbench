# Spoken Private Thought Texture

## Purpose

Prevent thought rows from becoming author commentary, moral summary, or review notes.
A VN thought row should sound like the viewpoint character's private mouth, not the writer's diagnosis.

## Failure Pattern

Bad thought rows often do one of these:

- Explain what a line means.
- Summarize the character's true nature.
- Turn a small action into a thesis.
- Use balanced abstract contrast.
- Sound too clean, too finished, too judge-like.

Example failure:

（原来她也会把真话藏在手续后面。）
（只是她藏得比我热闹。）

Why it fails: it names the mechanism from above. It is smart, but not alive.

## Positive Replacement

Thought should include at least one of:

- a hesitation: 啊、嗯、诶、……、等一下
- a self-correction: 算了、也许、大概、应该、吧
- a relaxation beat after tension: 嗯……当然？、好吧、先这样、当我没说
- a casual particle that belongs to the POV: 吧、嘛、啦、啊
- a private misread: 这是在谢我？还是在骗勺子？
- a tiny embarrassment: 别看，先别看她
- a practical worry hiding emotion: 勺子会不会太小
- a character's own vocabulary: 账、签收、额度、送检、广播、冰箱先生

Important: rhetorical questions are only one tool. Do not repair stiff psychology
by making every line a question. A thought row may loosen through a filler,
self-retraction, half joke, practical mask, or small particle.

## Rewrite Moves

1. Diagnosis -> private stutter
   - Bad: （她在害羞。）
   - Better: （啊。她把眼睛挪开了。这个时候看过去，好像很犯规。）

2. Theme explanation -> object worry
   - Bad: （他也想被记住。）
   - Better: （那张便利贴……要是明天还在的话，应该算她记得吧。）

3. Mechanism naming -> character vocabulary
   - Bad: （她也会把真话藏在手续后面。）
   - Better: （啊。她把谢谢写成贿赂了。这个人连道谢都要绕收银台吗。）

4. Pretty line -> playable pressure
   - Bad: （沉默像雨落下来。）
   - Better: （她再不说话，我就得先说了。可我说什么，签收成功？）

5. Stiff verdict -> tense-and-relax breath
   - Bad: （当然，它应该没有抗议功能。）
   - Better: （嗯……当然？它应该没有抗议功能。）

6. Clean inference -> loosened private mouth
   - Bad: （她在撤退。）
   - Better: （嗯。她把冰箱先生推到前线的准备动作，已经做到一半了。）

## Density

- One thought row can be a flinch.
- Two thought rows can be a flinch plus self-correction.
- Three thought rows need an incoming choice, route lock, or irreversible reply.
- Thoughts can use particles and unfinished grammar. They do not need to be polished.
- At least some thought rows in a cute/casual scene should contain relaxation
  texture: a filler, particle, retreat, half joke, or procedural mask softening
  the private pressure.

## Required Before Draft

For each viewpoint character, define private-thought diction:

- filler words
- safe vocabulary
- unsafe topic
- self-defense phrase
- what they call care when they refuse to say care
