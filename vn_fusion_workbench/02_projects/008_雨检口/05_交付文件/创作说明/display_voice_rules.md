# Display And Voice Rules

## Readable Draft Display

- Narration: plain line, no brackets.
- Spoken dialogue: `【角色名】台词`.
- Inner thought: `（短句）`; avoid long introspection.
- System/document text: `『系统或表格文字』`.
- Choice text: action phrase or spoken intent, no moral label.

## Engine CSV Display

- `speaker` carries the name box. Dialogue `text` should not include decorative brackets.
- Short commands may end without punctuation: `证件`, `姓`, `站外`, `拿着`.
- A line with punctuation must earn it through voice pressure.
- Narration can end with full punctuation because it is camera and procedure, not spoken breath.

## Project Voice Baseline

邵宁的句子从“岗位责任”里长出来：表、电话、倒查、签字、交班。他的关心先变成可执行动作。

岑遥的句子从“不能被完整登记”里长出来：先说物件，再绕名字；先刺人，再观察对方反应。

## Display Risks

- Do not make every dialogue line close with `。`.
- Do not use `……` as default sadness.
- Do not use `！` for ordinary emotion.
- Do not let narration become漂亮散文.
- Do not let readable brackets leak into engine CSV text.
